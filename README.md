#Website Security Checker

We are developing a comprehensive program designed to empower individuals with the ability to assess the safety and security of websites, utilizing SQL as the primary technology. This innovative solution will be accessible on various devices, including computers and mobile devices, providing users with a robust tool to protect themselves against scams, hacking, and other online threats. By leveraging this program, users will be equipped with a heightened sense of security, enabling them to navigate the online world with confidence and peace of mind.
